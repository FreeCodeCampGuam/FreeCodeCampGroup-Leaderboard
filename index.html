About FreeCodeCampGuam

In March of 2016, a group of people came together to start our local chapter, FreeCodeCampGuam, of the ever-growing FreeCodeCamp. FreeCodeCamp is "a friendly open-source community where you learn to code and help nonprofits." The members of FCCG came together with the goal to foster that ideal of community, collaboration, and charity, as well as to create a support network for all coders in Guam as our island's economy moves more and more toward technology.
